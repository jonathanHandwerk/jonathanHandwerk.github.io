Crepe

A crêpe or crepe (/kreɪp/ (About this soundlisten)[1] or /krɛp/, French: [kʁɛp] (About this soundlisten), Quebec French: [kʁaɪ̯p] (About this soundlisten)) is a type of very thin pancake. Crêpes are usually of two types: sweet crêpes (crêpes sucrées) and savoury galettes (crêpes salées). Crêpes are served with a variety of fillings, from the simplest with only sugar to flambéed crêpes Suzette or elaborate savoury galettes. Crêpes is from Brittany, a region in the northwest of France, the consumption is widespread in France, Belgium, the Netherlands, Canada, and many parts of Europe, North Africa, North America, Lebanon, Brazil and Argentina.
